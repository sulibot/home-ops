# Level 2 Mock Assessment Checklist

## Purpose

Prepare the repository, artifacts, owners, and evidence flow for an internal `CMMC Level 2` mock assessment.

## Scope

- SSP readiness
- policy and procedure completeness
- evidence index completeness
- interview readiness
- logistics and review workflow

## Owners

- `Ryan Lin` - executive sponsor and final readiness approver
- `Joshua Hornback` - primary technical lead for mock assessment preparation
- `Andy Volk` - business operations and compliance coordination lead for logistics and documentation flow
- `Sulaiman Ahmad` - delivery coordinator and automation support for repeatable mock assessment preparation

## Inputs and Dependencies

- tailored SSP
- complete domain documentation
- evidence index
- POA&M
- access to SMEs and system admins

## Checklist

### Documentation

- SSP reflects the actual environment
- all 14 domains have current documentation
- all documents have named owners and review dates

### Evidence

- every in-scope practice is mapped in the evidence index
- screenshots, logs, exports, and records are accessible
- stale or placeholder artifacts are removed or flagged

### People and Process

- control owners know their assigned practices
- SMEs are available for walkthroughs
- interview preparation notes exist for key domains

### Logistics

- evidence repository structure is stable
- naming conventions are consistent
- review schedule and issue log are defined

## Expected Outputs

- completed mock assessment checklist
- mock assessment readiness status
- list of blockers before formal assessment work

## Evidence or Artifacts Created

- readiness checklist
- blocker list
- mock assessment prep notes

## Definition of Done

- The team can run a mock assessment without scrambling to locate owners or evidence.
- All blockers are documented and assigned.
