# Level 2 Evidence Index

## Purpose

Create a practice-level evidence register for all in-scope `CMMC Level 2` practices.

## Scope

- all in-scope Level 2 practices
- tailored policies, procedures, technical proofs, review records, and operational outputs

## Columns

| Practice | Domain | Artifact Path | Evidence Type | Owner | Review Date | Status | Gap / POA&M Reference |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Fill in | Fill in | Fill in | policy/procedure/log/screenshot/export | Fill in | Fill in | not started | Fill in |

## Steps

1. Determine which Level 2 practices are in scope.
2. Map each practice to its primary artifact and proof source.
3. Record the owner and review date.
4. Mark gaps and link them to the POA&M or remediation tracker.
5. Reuse the index during the mock assessment.

## Expected Outputs

- Level 2 evidence register
- traceable practice-to-proof mapping

## Evidence or Artifacts Created

- evidence index
- review notes
- delta list for missing proof

## Definition of Done

- Every in-scope Level 2 practice has a mapped evidence path.
- Missing evidence is explicitly flagged and linked to remediation.
