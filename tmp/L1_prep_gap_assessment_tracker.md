# Level 1 Gap Assessment Tracker

## Purpose

Track the outcome of an internal `CMMC Level 1` gap assessment and the remediation work required to close deficiencies.

## Scope

- All 17 Level 1 practices
- All control validation findings

## Tracker Fields

| Practice | Finding | Severity | Remediation Action | Owner | Target Date | Evidence Needed | Status |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Fill in | Fill in | low/medium/high | Fill in | Fill in | Fill in | Fill in | not started |

## Steps

1. Assess each Level 1 practice against the current artifact and operating evidence.
2. Record deficiencies, missing artifacts, stale artifacts, and failed control checks.
3. Assign an owner and due date to every finding.
4. Re-validate evidence once remediation is complete.

## Expected Outputs

- Level 1 gap tracker
- Remediation list with dates and owners

## Evidence or Artifacts Created

- Assessment worksheet
- Remediation closure records

## Definition of Done

- Every Level 1 gap has an owner, action, and due date.
- Closed findings have updated evidence linked in the tracker.
